Ek Pehal, an youth-led initiative by the students of IISER Kolkata, empowers children of the underprivileged and rock-bottom strata of the society. With a vision in mind, as a young movement of enthused dynamic crowd, we’re sheltering to discover the true strengths and unleash the full potential and capability of the children living in and around the villages of Nadia, West Bengal. We believe a small combined investment of knowledge for a noble cause can bring wonderful solutions to regional problems and global solutions.
